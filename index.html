CCCAM WebUI

Update clines on your QVIART Unic decoder.

Based on the original idea of NeTDaRk & Popov.
  1. Write below the IP of your decoder.
  2. Select a way to introduce your information: using a text file, copy them into the text area, or use the available online providers to get clines.
  3. Click on Process button. Then, a new row per cline will be shown below.
  4. At this moment, you can update the information if you want.
  5. Click on the Update button of those rows you want to send. Or click on Update All button to send them all to your decoder.
